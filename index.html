Our Story

More Than a Diner.
It's Home.

Locals Diner was born from a simple belief: a meal should feel like a warm hug from someone who genuinely cares. From the first cup of Beanshooter Coffee to the last bite of homemade pie, every detail is designed to make you feel like family.

We're a family-owned breakfast and lunch spot in the heart of Dracut, MA — and we take that seriously. Our portions are generous, our ingredients are fresh, and our smiles are always free. Whether you're a first-timer or a regular who has their "usual," you'll always leave satisfied.
